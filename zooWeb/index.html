About us

You can discover ZooParc

Step into our magical 70-hectare wonderland, where 2,000 incredible animals from 200 species are waiting to meet you! From our adorable giant pandas to majestic lions, every corner of ZooParc is bursting with life and adventure. Imagine standing eye-to-eye with a gentle Asian elephant, watching bald eagles soar overhead, or marveling at the vibrant colors of our tiny poisonous frogs. Our playful orangutans and lazy sloth bears will steal your heart, while our elusive wild deer might just play hide-and-seek with you! At ZooParc, we're not just a home for animals - we're a family of passionate animal lovers, dedicated to creating a better world for wildlife. Every visit is a chance to learn, explore, and fall in love with nature. Our carefully crafted habitats aren't just enclosures; they're windows into the amazing diversity of our planet. So come on in, become part of our wild family, and join us on an unforgettable journey. Together, we'll create magical memories, inspire a love for wildlife, and work hand-in-hand to protect the wonders of the animal kingdom. Are you ready for your ZooParc adventure?

Our Amazing ZooParc Volunteer Family community

Meet the heart and soul of ZooParc - our incredible volunteer family! These everyday heroes bring the magic of our zoo to life with their boundless enthusiasm and love for animals. From sharing fascinating animal facts on guided tours to lending a helping hand behind the scenes, our volunteers sprinkle joy and wonder throughout the park. Their passion is contagious, turning each visit into an unforgettable adventure. They're not just helpers; they're the friendly faces that make ZooParc feel like home. Come and experience the warmth and dedication of our volunteer family - they can't wait to share their love of nature with you

Savor the Flavors of ZooParc

Hungry for adventure? Let your taste buds join the fun at ZooParc! Dotted around our animal wonderland, you'll find cozy nooks and crannies serving up delicious treats. Fancy a quick bite to refuel your excitement? Or how about a leisurely family picnic with the gentle rustling of leaves and distant animal calls as your soundtrack? From mouthwatering meals to grab-and-go goodies, we've got flavors to make everyone's tummy happy. So come on, take a culinary safari with us - where every meal is seasoned with a sprinkle of wildlife magic
